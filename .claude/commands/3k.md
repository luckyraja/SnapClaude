# 3k Token Evaluation Command

Evaluate whether a Marvel Snap card is worth purchasing with 
000 tokens based on its current meta relevance and deck versatility.

## Card to Evaluate:
{CARD_NAME}

## What to search for:
- Current Marvel Snap meta analysis for {CARD_NAME} (December 2025)
- Deck archetypes that use {CARD_NAME}
- Win rates and competitive viability of {CARD_NAME}
- Recent tournament results featuring {CARD_NAME}
- Professional player opinions on {CARD_NAME}
- Tier list rankings for {CARD_NAME}

## What to analyze and provide:

### 1. Meta Relevance (Current State)
- Is the card seeing play in top-tier meta decks?
- Recent buffs/nerfs or changes affecting the card
- Current popularity in the competitive scene

### 2. Deck Archetypes
- List all viable deck archetypes that use this card
- Is it a core card or flex slot in these decks?
- How many different decks can use it effectively?

### 3. Future Potential
- Any upcoming synergies or cards that might boost its value?
- Is it likely to remain relevant or fall off?

### 4. Collection Value
- Is it a unique effect or replaceable?
- Does it enable specific strategies not available otherwise?

### 5. Final Recommendation
**Score: X/10**

Provide a clear score out of 10 with reasoning:
- 9-10: Must-buy, meta-defining or highly versatile
- 7-8: Strong choice, solid in multiple decks
- 5-6: Good in specific archetypes, situational
- 3-4: Niche use, only if you love the archetype
- 1-2: Skip, not worth 3k tokens currently

Include a brief summary explaining the score and whether to buy now, wait, or skip entirely.
